### 1.3 品牌故事

**The Meridian Story**

For thousands of years, the world's great civilizations mapped the night sky, tracked the turning of years, and built systems to understand one fundamental question: *why are some people born to certain patterns, and what does that mean for how they should live?*

The Chinese sages who developed Bazi, Zi Wei Dou Shu, and Qi Men Dun Jia were not superstitionists. They were observationalists — rigorous, systematic thinkers who spent centuries correlating birth timing, elemental cycles, and human behavior. What they built were frameworks. Models. The 16th-century equivalent of a personality matrix.

Meridian was created because these frameworks are extraordinarily precise — and almost entirely inaccessible to the modern Western mind.

Not because they're too complex. But because they've never been translated well.

Every Meridian report takes your birth data and runs it through four to six of these ancient analytical systems — not in isolation, but in cross-reference. Where they converge, the insight sharpens. Where they diverge, we note the tension. The result is a document unlike anything a standard personality test can produce: deep, specific, and surprisingly accurate in ways that take most readers by surprise.

We don't tell you what will happen. We show you what you're made of.

The rest is yours to navigate.

### 2.5 为什么这套色彩适合欧美高端用户

**哑光金 `#C4A96B` 的使用逻辑：**
欧美高端品牌对金色的使用极为克制，且倾向"古董金/铜金"而非"闪亮金"。
参考：Aesop的铜色包装，Dieter Rams设计的黄铜细节，Tom Ford的哑光金标。
金色在西方奢侈品语境中= 精准、永久、经典，而非俗气。

**Ivory背景 `#F7F5F0` 的使用逻辑：**
纯白让人联想到医疗/科技。米白让人联想到手工纸张、皮革装订书籍、建筑师的绘图纸。
Aesop和The Row都用接近这个色值的背景。它向用户传递"这是一件工艺品"的信号。

**极简的颜色数量：**
MasterClass：黑+金。Notion：白+黑。Aesop：棕+米。
颜色克制 = 信心。"我不需要用颜色来吸引你的注意。内容会说话。"

**禁用紫色的理由：**
紫色在英语圈文化中强关联：神秘主义、塔罗、水晶healing——恰好是我们需要规避的品牌联想。

### 3.4 FAQ

```
Q: What frameworks do these reports actually use?

A: Our reports draw on classical Chinese analytical systems: Bazi (Four Pillars 
of Destiny), Zi Wei Dou Shu (Purple Star Astrology), Qi Men Dun Jia, Liu Yao, 
and classical Feng Shui — depending on the report tier. These are complex, 
structured analytical frameworks with thousands of years of development behind them. 
We translate their output into clear, modern English.

Q: Is this the same as Western astrology or tarot?

A: No. These are distinct systems with different methodological foundations. 
They work with birth date, time, and location data to generate structured analyses 
of character patterns, tendencies, and cycles. The output is more specific and 
internally consistent than most Western astrological approaches, which is why 
practitioners spend years training in them.

Q: How accurate are these reports?

A: Accuracy is subjective — but we consistently hear from customers that the 
reports are "uncomfortably specific." What we can say objectively: the frameworks 
we use are deterministic (same input = same output), not probabilistic guessing. 
The interpretation is the skilled part. We treat these as mirrors, not oracles: 
useful for reflection, not for decision-making on their own.

Q: What do I need to provide?

A: Full birth date (day/month/year), birth time (as precise as possible — 
to the hour is fine), and birth location (city and country). 
If you don't have your birth time, note "unknown" — we'll note the impact 
on the analysis and work with available data.

Q: How long does delivery take?

A: 24–48 hours from receiving your completed intake form. 
Tier 3 (Master Blueprint) may take up to 72 hours given the additional 
analytical systems involved.

Q: What format are the reports in?

A: High-resolution PDF, designed for screen reading and home printing. 
20–50 pages depending on tier. Not editable.

Q: Can I buy this as a gift?

A: Yes — and it's a particularly good gift for milestone birthdays, 
major transitions, or anyone serious about personal development. 
Note "gift" at checkout and include the recipient's birth information. 
Report goes to your email for you to deliver personally.

Q: Are refunds available?

A: Because every report is custom-created using your specific birth data, 
we cannot offer refunds once a report has been delivered. If you're 
unsatisfied, message us directly — we will genuinely try to make it right.

Q: Is this a spiritual or religious product?

A: No. The frameworks are analytical and historical. You don't need to 
hold any particular belief system to read or benefit from the reports. 
Many of our customers are skeptical by nature — they find the reports 
useful precisely because the frameworks operate on structural logic, 
not faith.
```
